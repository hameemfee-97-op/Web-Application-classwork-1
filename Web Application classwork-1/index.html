Discover Dhaka

Located along the banks of the Buriganga River, Dhaka is the heart of Bangladesh — home to over 20 million people, centuries-old landmarks, buzzing markets, and a fast-paced modern life. Whether you're a lover of history, food, or architecture, Dhaka has something for everyone. Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh, is one of the most densely populated cities in the world. It is known for its vibrant street life, historical architecture, and as the commercial heart of the country. Dhaka blends rich Mughal heritage with modern growth. Landmarks like Lalbagh Fort, Ahsan Manzil, and the National Parliament showcase its unique cultural and political identity. Despite its rapid urbanization, the city maintains a deep connection to its traditions, cuisine, and community.
